SEVENTEEN

SEVENTEEN is a South Korean boy group formed by Pledis Entertainment on 26 May 2015. The group consists of 13 members. There are S.Coups, Jeonghan, Joshua, Jun, Hoshi, Wonwoo, Woozi, DK, Mingyu, The 8, Seungkwan, Vernon, and Dino. They are called SEVENTEEN or SVT because the group performs as one group and the members are divided into three sub-units 'Performance Unit', 'Vocal Unit', and 'Hip-hop Unit'. SEVENTEEN is famous for being called 'Idol Producing' because they produce their own songs and choreography.
